Linear Algebra is crucial for BTech CSE students as it forms the foundation of many core computer science fields. It is widely used in machine learning, data science, and computer graphics, helping in dimensionality reduction, image processing, and optimization tasks. Concepts like matrices, eigenvalues, and vector spaces are fundamental in algorithms, cryptography, and network theory. Linear algebra also plays a key role in artificial intelligence, powering techniques such as neural networks and principal component analysis (PCA). Mastery of these concepts enables students to solve complex problems in areas like data analysis, computational geometry, and scientific computing.
