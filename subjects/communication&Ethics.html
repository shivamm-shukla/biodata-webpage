Communication and ethics are vital for B.Tech CSE students as they bridge the gap between technical expertise and professional success. Effective communication ensures clear articulation of complex ideas, seamless teamwork, and successful project execution. It is essential for writing code documentation, presenting projects, and collaborating across diverse teams. Ethics, on the other hand, guide students in responsibly handling data, respecting intellectual property, and making moral decisions in software development. Upholding ethical standards fosters trust, accountability, and legal compliance, preparing students for responsible roles in the tech industry. Both skills ensure holistic professional development and long-term success.
